# Recruix.ai Brand Differentiation Guide

How to make Recruix stand out from Jobright.ai and other AI job platforms.

---

## Current Overlap (What to Change)

| Aspect | Jobright.ai | Recruix (current) |
|--------|-------------|-------------------|
| Primary color | Purple/indigo | Purple/indigo (#4f46e5) |
| Tagline | "AI Job Search Copilot" | "AI-Powered Career Copilot" |
| Accent | Teal/cyan | Teal (#14b8a6) |
| Hero message | "No More Solo Job Hunting" | "Land your dream job with AI precision" |
| Features | Autofill, matches, resume, referrals | AI Match, Autofill, AI Copilot |
| Vibe | Corporate, testimonial-heavy | Generic SaaS |

---

## 1. Visual Identity (Quick Wins)

### Option A: Warm & Human (Recommended)
- **Primary:** Amber/coral (#ea580c or #f97316) — optimism, warmth, stands out from cold AI purple
- **Accent:** Deep teal (#0d9488) — trust, balance
- **Feel:** Approachable, human, less "corporate AI"

### Option B: Dark & Tech-Focused
- **Primary:** Slate/navy (#0f172a)
- **Accent:** Electric mint (#5eead4) or lime (#84cc16)
- **Feel:** Developer-focused, modern, premium

### Option C: Fresh & Minimal
- **Primary:** Deep forest green (#15803d)
- **Accent:** Cream (#fef3c7) or warm white
- **Feel:** Growth, calm, trustworthy

### Typography
Replace Inter with something more distinctive:
- **Syne** — geometric, modern, memorable
- **Outfit** — rounded, friendly, readable
- **Sora** — tech-forward, clean
- **Geist** — Vercel-style, developer-friendly

---

## 2. Positioning & Messaging

### Jobright’s Angle
Broad “AI copilot,” autofill, referrals, 1.25M users.

### Recruix’s Angle (Pick One)

**A. "Match First, Apply Later"**
- Focus on match % before applying
- "Stop applying blindly. See your fit first."
- Lead with Resume Match (paste JD → get score)
- Quality over quantity

**B. "For Students & New Grads"**
- You already say "12,400+ students and new grads"
- Make it explicit: "The job matcher built for your first big role"
- Less overwhelming, more guided

**C. "Your Resume Does the Talking"**
- Resume-first flow
- "Upload once. Get scored on every job."
- Simpler than "autofill + referrals + copilot"

### Suggested Hero (Option A)
- **Headline:** "Know your fit before you apply"
- **Sub:** "Paste any job. Get your match score in seconds. Apply only when it matters."
- **CTA:** "Try Match Score" (instead of "Get started")

---

## 3. Layout & Structure

- **Less sections** — Jobright has hero, stats, features grid, testimonials, FAQ, search. Recruix can be: hero → one strong feature (Match) → live jobs → CTA.
- **Single focus** — One primary action: "See your match" or "Search jobs"
- **No testimonial carousel** — Or use 1–2 short quotes instead of a long slider
- **Different nav** — "Match" and "Jobs" instead of "Features" and "How it works"

---

## 4. Unique Features to Highlight

| Recruix Has | Jobright Emphasizes | Recruix Should Lead With |
|-------------|---------------------|--------------------------|
| Resume Match (paste JD → score) | Autofill | **Resume Match** — more unique |
| Match % on job cards | Match % | Same, but frame as "fit before apply" |
| AI Copilot (Aria) | Orion | Give Aria a distinct voice/personality |
| Applied tracking | Referrals | Simpler, more transparent |
| Saved jobs | — | Good differentiator |

---

## 5. Tone & Personality

- **Jobright:** Corporate, feature-heavy, social proof–driven
- **Recruix:** Calmer, more direct, less hype
- **Aria:** Friendly but concise; avoid generic "AI assistant" tone
